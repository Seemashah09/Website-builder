STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

How to Create a Free Website

Follow these 6 simple steps to build and publish your professional website today.

1

Create a Free Canva Account

Go to canva.com and sign up for a free account. You can use your email, Google, or Facebook to register.

💡 Tip: Canva is completely free to use. The AI website builder is included in the free plan!

2

Ask Canva AI to Generate Your Website

Use Canva's AI assistant to describe your website. Say something like: "Create a modern business website for a bakery" and watch the magic happen!

🎯 Pro Tip: Be specific about your industry, colors, and style for better results.

3

Copy the HTML Code

Once satisfied with your design, export or copy the HTML code from Canva. This is the code that makes your website work.

📋 Note: Don't worry about understanding the code — you just need to copy and paste it!

4

Create a Free GitHub Account & Repository

Go to github.com and create a free account. Then create a new repository (think of it as a folder for your website files).

🔒 Why GitHub? It's free, reliable, and can host your website for free with GitHub Pages!

5

Paste Code in index.html

Create a new file called index.html in your repository and paste the HTML code you copied from Canva. Save the file.

✅ Important: The file must be named exactly "index.html" — this is the main page of your website.

6

Connect Your Custom Domain

Enable GitHub Pages in your repository settings. Then connect your custom domain (like yourbusiness.com) to make it official!

🌐 Bonus: You can also use the free GitHub URL (username.github.io) if you don't have a domain yet.

🎉

Congratulations!

Your website is now live on the internet!

How to Set Up Free Email with Zoho Mail

1

Go to Zoho Mail Free Plan

Visit zoho.com/mail and click on the "Free Plan" option. The free tier includes everything you need for a professional email.

💡 Free Plan Includes: 1 GB storage, web access, mobile apps, and up to 5 users!

2

Enter Your Domain Name

Enter the domain you want to use for your email (e.g., yourbusiness.com). This is the same domain as your website.

🌐 Note: You need to own a domain name. You can purchase one from Namecheap, Google Domains, or similar registrars.

3

Verify Your Domain

Zoho will ask you to verify ownership of your domain. Add a TXT record or CNAME record to your domain's DNS settings.

⚙️ How to: Log into your domain registrar, go to DNS settings, and add the record Zoho provides. It usually takes 5-30 minutes to verify.

4

Create Your Email Account

Once verified, create your first email address. Popular choices include:

contact@
hello@
info@
yourname@

✅ Example: contact@buildsitefree.com is our professional email address!

5

Connect Email to Your Website

Add your professional email to your website's Contact page, footer, or use it for notifications and form submissions.

🔗 Pro Tip: Set up email forwarding to your personal email so you never miss a message!
